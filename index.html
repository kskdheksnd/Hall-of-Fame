Hall of Fame

Legends who shaped football history.

Football Icons

Pelé

Title: The King of Football

Widely considered one of the greatest players ever, Pelé revolutionized football with his unmatched skill, vision, and success with Brazil.

Diego Maradona

Title: The Golden Boy

Known for extraordinary dribbling and creativity, Maradona's "Hand of God" and solo goal vs England remain iconic moments in football history.

Zinedine Zidane

Title: The Maestro

A midfield genius whose elegance and control shaped France’s World Cup and Euro victories. Later became a legendary coach for Real Madrid.

Lionel Messi

Title: The Magician

One of the most decorated players of all time, known for his precise dribbling, vision, and historic 2022 World Cup victory.
